1-Day Scenarios

Dataset Overview
This dataset contains 200 synthetic daily scenarios of distributed wind and solar power generation, designed for power distribution system analysis and optimization studies. Each scenario represents a complete 24-hour period with 15-minute resolution data (96 time points per day).

Dataset Structure
The dataset consists of:
200 individual CSV files: scenario_001.csv to scenario_200.csv
1 label file: scenario_labels.csv - Contains the classification labels for each scenario

Individual Scenario Files (scenario_XXX.csv)
Each scenario file contains the following columns:
scenario_id: Scenario identifier (1-200)
timestamp: Date and time in 15-minute intervals (96 points per day)
node_22_wind: Wind power output at Node 22 (normalized 0-1)
node_25_wind: Wind power output at Node 25 (normalized 0-1)
node_18_PV: Solar PV power output at Node 18 (normalized 0-1)
node_33_PV: Solar PV power output at Node 33 (normalized 0-1)

Label File (scenario_labels.csv)
Contains the following columns:
scenario_index: Scenario identifier (1-200)
wind_label: Wind power generation level classification (0, 1, 2)
pv_label: Solar power generation level classification (0, 1, 2)

Wind Power Labels (wind_label)
The wind power scenarios are classified into three categories based on daily generation patterns:

Label 0 - Low Wind Generation
Characterized by consistently low wind speeds
Daily average power output: Low range
Typical of calm weather conditions
Lower variability in power output


Label 1 - Medium Wind Generation
Characterized by moderate wind speeds
Daily average power output: Medium range
Typical of normal wind conditions
Moderate variability in power output


Label 2 - High Wind Generation
Characterized by strong wind speeds
Daily average power output: High range
Typical of windy weather conditions
Higher variability in power output

Solar Power Labels (pv_label)
The solar power scenarios are classified into three categories based on weather conditions and irradiance levels:

Label 0 - High Solar Generation (Clear/Sunny Weather)
Clear sky conditions with high solar irradiance
Maximum daily solar power output
Smooth, bell-shaped daily generation curve
Minimal cloud interference


Label 1 - Medium Solar Generation (Partly Cloudy Weather)
Partly cloudy conditions with moderate solar irradiance
Medium daily solar power output
Irregular generation patterns due to cloud movements
Intermittent power fluctuations


Label 2 - Low Solar Generation (Overcast/Rainy Weather)
Overcast, rainy, or snowy weather conditions
Low solar irradiance and minimal power generation
Consistently low output throughout the day
Severely limited by weather conditions
